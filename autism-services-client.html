Overview

I led the design and development efforts in collaboration with internal teams and the client's Senior Digital Marketing Specialist to build a fresh website that focuses on clear organization, useful information, and organic audience growth.


We also produced an SEO landing page campaign consisting of 50 pages, with each page targeting a specific facility location. I designed and built these pages to cater to potential clients looking for more information and also produce signifcant results when crawled by search engine bots.

Project Goals

The key objectives for the project were:

  • Provide detailed information about the location facilities and services offered
  • Drive new traffic to the site by use of SEO best practices and quality, helpful content
  • Include obvious contact options for users to gather more information or to schedule services
  • Implement an accessible, responsive, and user-friendly platform that promotes ease of use and caters to those with disabilities

Client Vision & Brand

My first step was to conduct an in-depth discussion with the client to understand their business, target audience, and vision for the site. This helped identify their brand message, values, and the overall aesthetic they wanted to convey. Given that their target audience is mostly parents looking for therapy services for their child, it was important that the site feel both approachable and professional, reflecting their credibility and kind approach.

Design Process

Navigation Structure & Organization



My goal while creating the navigation structure was to provide obvious links to essential information, most importantly, services and facility locations.


While we aimed to expand the site, we prioritized featuring the most critical information and clear CTAs on the homepage to reduce click count, accommodating users that are ready to reach out.

Style Guide & Visual Design


The client had a playful, bright color scheme tied to their logo, so I incorporated these colors throughout the design as accents, ensuring they were bold yet balanced. I maintained a minimal yet fun style to prevent overwhelm and to balance a child-friendly and professional look. I focused on clean typography and plenty of white space to enhance readability.

Wireframes & Prototypes









I started by sketching low-fidelity wireframes to outline the website's structure. After team approval, I developed prototypes using Figma to provide a more tangible experience of the design and functionality.

Development

Technical Considerations

I chose WordPress with an Elementor theme for its flexibility and ease of use. This ensured that the client could manage their website in the future without needing extensive technical knowledge.


Development involved:

  • Performance optimization: Image compression, CSS/JS minification, and lazy loading for fast load times
  • Accessibility considerations: I added an accessiBE extension to make sure the site is certified WCAG compliant
  • SEO improvements: On-page SEO techniques like meta descriptions, optimized titles, quality content, and schema markup
  • Mobile responsiveness: Ensuring that the site delivered a consistent, fully functional experience across all screen sizes
  • Database storage for form submissions: Capturing and securely storing form data for easy access and management

Testing & Launch

Before launching the site, I conducted rigorous testing:

  • Browser compatibility across Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge
  • Performance testing using Google Lighthouse, with a focus on improving load times and page speed
  • Accessibility checks to meet WCAG standards, ensuring the site was inclusive for all users

After thorough testing, the site went live. I also provided the client with training and documentation to manage and update the website independently.

Results
& Conclusion

The final website achieved all the set goals:

  • New information architecture: The new website structure encouraged site exploration, helping new or returning users learn about the company and their offerings.
  • SEO optimizations: Overall best practices along with the implemented SEO campaign resulted in a 40-45% increase in new website users in the first 90 days.
  • Clear, compelling CTAs: The addition of numerous, obvious contact options encourages users to reach out whenever they desire to.
  • Accessible, responsive, and user-friendly: The website functioned seamlessly across devices, utilized an intuitive flow, and included various accessibility options.
